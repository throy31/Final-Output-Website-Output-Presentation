About the Course

Nestled in the heart of rolling mountains and pristine wilderness, The Wednesday Golf Club represents more than just a round of golf. It’s a journey through nature’s masterpiece. Our championship course winds through ancient pine forests and meandering streams, where every hole tells a story of the land’s natural beauty.
